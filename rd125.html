RD - 125


The Yamaha RD-125 was a small-displacement two-stroke motorcycle that gained popularity for its accessible power and easy maneuverability. Introduced in the 1970s, it was ideal for new riders and those looking for a commuter bike with a sporty edge. The RD-125's combination of light weight and responsive handling made it a fun and practical ride.
