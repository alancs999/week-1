RD - 400


The Yamaha RD-400, introduced in the late 1970s, was a powerful two-stroke motorcycle known for its impressive acceleration and sporty handling. It became a favorite among riders for its balance of performance and everyday usability. The RD-400 also featured disc brakes, a significant advancement for its time.
