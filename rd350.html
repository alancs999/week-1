RD - 350


The Yamaha RD-350, released in the early 1970s, was a powerful two-stroke motorcycle renowned for its rapid acceleration and strong performance. It quickly became a favorite among racing enthusiasts and street riders alike. The RD-350 was also known for its lightweight frame and excellent handling, making it a versatile and thrilling ride.
